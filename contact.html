Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer complimentary white-glove delivery within Lesotho. International shipping is available with tracking and insurance. Delivery times vary from 3-7 business days for in-stock items and 4-8 weeks for bespoke creations.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 14-day return policy on ready-to-ship items. Bespoke and custom-made pieces are final sale, as they are crafted specifically for you. All returns are complimentary with white-glove pickup service.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we partner with select financial institutions to offer flexible payment plans. Please contact our concierge to discuss options tailored to your needs.

How do I care for my Luxe Haven pieces?

Each piece comes with a detailed care guide. For furniture, we recommend professional cleaning. For textiles, dry cleaning is recommended. Our concierge team is always available to advise on proper care.

Can I request custom sizing?

Absolutely! Many of our collections can be customized to your exact specifications. Visit our Bespoke page or contact our design team to discuss your vision.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, Luxe Haven gift cards are available in denominations from M 1,000 to M 50,000. Perfect for special occasions. Contact our concierge to purchase.
